SR-ORG:6903

USA Contiguous Lambert Azmuthal Equal Area (USGS-EDC) (Google it)

Common sphere-based projection for gridded (2 x 2 degree tiles) data such as AVHRR Biweekly Composites and Daymet data. There are many variations with a modified latitude of center or longitude of center or both.

  • WGS84 Bounds: -142.3828, 5.9658, -54.4922, 55.7766
  • Projected Bounds: -4785988.5722, -3183771.5468, 5107621.8557, 2019494.2545
